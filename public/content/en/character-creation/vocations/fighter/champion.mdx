# Champion

_Pursue Physical Excellence in Combat._

A Champion focuses on the development of martial prowess in a relentless pursuit of victory. Champions combine rigorous training with physical excellence to deal devastating blows, withstand peril, and garner glory. Whether in athletic contests or bloody battle, Champions strive for the crown of the victor.

---

## 3rd Level – Improved Critical

Your attack rolls with **weapons** and **Unarmed Strikes** score a **Critical Hit** on a roll of **19 or 20**.

---

## 3rd Level – Remarkable Athlete

- You have **Advantage** on **Initiative rolls** and **Strength (Athletics) checks**.
- Additionally, when you score a **Critical Hit**, you may move up to **half your Speed** without provoking **Opportunity Attacks**.

---

## 7th Level – Additional Fighting Style

You gain an additional **[Fighting Style feat](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:all)** of your choice.

---

## 10th Level – Heroic Warrior

The thrill of battle drives you toward victory.  
At the **start of your turn**, if you do not have **Heroic Inspiration**, you may grant it to yourself.

---

## 15th Level – Superior Critical

Your attack rolls with **weapons** and **Unarmed Strikes** score a **Critical Hit** on a roll of **18–20**.

---

## 18th Level – Survivor

You attain the pinnacle of resilience in battle, gaining the following benefits:

### Defy Death

- You have **Advantage** on **Death Saving Throws**.
- When you roll **18–20** on a Death Saving Throw, you gain the benefits of rolling a **20**.

---

### Heroic Rally

At the **start of each of your turns**, if you are **Bloodied** and have at least **1 Hit Point**, you regain **5 + your Constitution modifier** hit points.
