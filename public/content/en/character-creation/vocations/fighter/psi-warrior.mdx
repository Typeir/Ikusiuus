# Psi-Warrior

_Augment Physical Might with Psionic Power._

Psi Warriors awaken the power of their minds to augment their physical might. They harness psionic power to infuse their weapon strikes, lash out with telekinetic energy, and create barriers of mental force.

---

## 3rd Level – Psionic Power

You harbor a wellspring of psionic energy within yourself. It is represented by your **Psionic Energy Dice**, which fuel the powers you gain from this subclass.

##### Psi Warrior Energy Dice

| Fighter Level | Die Size | Number of Dice |
| ------------- | -------- | -------------- |
| 3rd           | d6       | 4              |
| 5th           | d8       | 6              |
| 9th           | d8       | 8              |
| 11th          | d10      | 8              |
| 13th          | d10      | 10             |
| 17th          | d12      | 12             |

- Some features expend a Psionic Energy Die as specified in their description.
- If you have no dice remaining, you cannot use features that require them.
- You regain **1 die on a Short Rest** and **all dice on a Long Rest**.

---

### Protective Field

When you or a creature you can see within **30 feet** takes damage, you may use your **Reaction** to expend a Psionic Energy Die. Roll the die and reduce the damage taken by the result + your **Intelligence modifier** (minimum reduction of 1).

---

### Psionic Strike

Once per turn, immediately after you hit a target within **30 feet** with a weapon attack, you may expend a Psionic Energy Die to deal extra **Force damage** equal to the die rolled + your **Intelligence modifier**.

---

### Telekinetic Movement

As a **Magic action**, choose a **willing creature** or **loose object** within **30 feet**.

- Move the target up to **30 feet** to an unoccupied space you can see.
- You may also move a **Tiny object** to or from your hand.

Once you use this feature, you cannot do so again until you finish a **Short or Long Rest**, unless you expend a Psionic Energy Die (no action required) to restore its use.

---

## 7th Level – Telekinetic Adept

You have mastered advanced techniques of telekinetic combat.

---

### Psi-Powered Leap

As a **Bonus Action**, gain a **Fly Speed equal to twice your Speed** until the end of your turn.

Once you use this feature, you cannot do so again until you finish a **Short or Long Rest**, unless you expend a Psionic Energy Die (no action required) to restore its use.

---

### Telekinetic Thrust

When you deal damage with **Psionic Strike**, you may force the target to make a **Strength saving throw** (DC = 8 + your Proficiency Bonus + your Intelligence modifier).

| Result      | Effect                                                                                   |
| ----------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Fail**    | The target is either **knocked prone** or **pushed 10 feet horizontally** (your choice). |
| **Success** | No additional effect.                                                                    |

---

## 10th Level – Guarded Mind

- You gain **Resistance to Psychic damage**.
- At the start of your turn, if you are **Charmed** or **Frightened**, you may expend a Psionic Energy Die (no action required) to end all effects causing those conditions.

---

## 15th Level – Bulwark of Force

As a **Bonus Action**, choose a number of creatures within **30 feet** (up to your **Intelligence modifier**, minimum 1). Each target gains **Half Cover** for **1 minute**, or until you become **Incapacitated**.

Once you use this feature, you cannot do so again until you finish a **Long Rest**, unless you expend a Psionic Energy Die (no action required) to restore its use.

---

## 18th Level – Telekinetic Master

You always have the **[Telekinesis](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:telekinesis)** spell prepared. You may cast it:

- **Without a spell slot or components.**
- **Using Intelligence as your spellcasting ability.**

While maintaining concentration on Telekinesis, you may make **one weapon attack as a Bonus Action** on each of your turns.

Once you cast Telekinesis this way, you cannot do so again until you finish a **Long Rest**, unless you expend a Psionic Energy Die (no action required) to restore its use.
