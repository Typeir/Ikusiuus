# Path of the Wild Heart

_Walk in Community with the Animal World._

Barbarians who follow the Path of the Wild Heart view themselves as kin to animals. These Barbarians learn magical means to communicate with animals, and their Rage heightens their connection to the animal world as it fills them with supernatural might.

---

## 3rd Level – Animal Speaker

You can cast **[Beast Sense](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:beast-sense)** and **[Speak with Animals](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:speak-with-animals)** as **Rituals**.  
Your **spellcasting ability** for these spells is **Wisdom**.

---

## 3rd Level – Rage of the Wilds

When you activate your **Rage**, choose one of the following options:

### Bear

While your Rage is active, you have **Resistance** to all damage types **except Force, Necrotic, Psychic, and Radiant**.

---

### Eagle

When you activate your Rage, you can take the **Disengage** and **Dash** actions as part of the same **Bonus Action**.

While your Rage is active, you can take a **Bonus Action** on each of your turns to use both **Disengage** and **Dash**.

---

### Wolf

While your Rage is active, **allies have Advantage on attack rolls** against enemies that are **within 5 feet of you**.

---

## 6th Level – Aspect of the Wilds

You gain one of the following features.  
You may change your choice whenever you finish a **Long Rest**.

### Owl

You gain **Darkvision with a range of 60 feet**.  
If you already have Darkvision, its range increases by **60 feet**.

---

### Panther

You gain a **Climb Speed equal to your Speed**.

---

### Salmon

You gain a **Swim Speed equal to your Speed**.

---

## 10th Level – Nature Speaker

You can cast **[Commune with Nature](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:commune-with-nature)** as a **Ritual**.  
Your **spellcasting ability** for this spell is **Wisdom**.

---

## 14th Level – Power of the Wilds

When you activate your **Rage**, choose one of the following options:

### Falcon

While your Rage is active, you gain a **Fly Speed equal to your Speed** if you are **not wearing armor**.

---

### Lion

While your Rage is active, **enemies within 5 feet of you have Disadvantage on attack rolls** against targets other than you or another Barbarian using this feature.

---

### Ram

While your Rage is active, when you **hit a Large or smaller creature** with a melee attack, you may cause it to become **Prone**.
