# War Domain

_Inspire Valor and Smite Foes._

The War Domain encompasses all aspects of battle. Some War Clerics fight in defense of the innocent, others glorify conquest, and still others treat war as a neutral force of change. Gods of this domain include champions of honor and chivalry, destructive gods of pillage, and impartial deities who support warriors in all circumstances.

Clerics of the War Domain inspire valor and offer acts of violence as prayers to their gods.

---

## 3rd Level – Guided Strike

When you or a creature within **30 feet** misses with an **attack roll**, you can expend one use of **Channel Divinity** to give that roll a **+10 bonus**, potentially turning it into a hit.

- If you use this feature on another creature’s attack roll, you must use your **Reaction**.

## 3rd Level – War Priest

As a **Bonus Action**, you can make **one weapon attack** or **Unarmed Strike**.

- You may use this feature **a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier** (minimum of once).
- You regain all uses when you finish a **Short or Long Rest**.

---

## 6th Level – War God’s Blessing

You may expend a use of **Channel Divinity** to cast:

- **[Shield of Faith](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:shield-of-faith)**
- **[Spiritual Weapon](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:spiritual-weapon)**

When you cast either spell this way:

- It **does not require Concentration**.
- The spell lasts for **1 minute**, unless you cast the same spell again, become **Incapacitated**, or die.

---

## 17th Level – Avatar of Battle

You gain **Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage**.
