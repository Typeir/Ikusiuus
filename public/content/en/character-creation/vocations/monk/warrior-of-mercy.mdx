# Warrior of Mercy

_Manipulate Forces of Life and Death._

Warriors of Mercy manipulate the life force of others. These Monks are wandering physicians, yet they bring a swift end to their enemies. They often wear masks, presenting themselves as faceless bringers of life and death.

---

## 3rd Level – Hand of Harm

Once per turn, when you hit a creature with an **Unarmed Strike** and deal damage, you may expend **1 Focus Point** to deal additional **Necrotic damage** equal to:

**1 × Martial Arts die + Wisdom modifier**

---

## 3rd Level – Hand of Healing

As a **Magic Action**, you may expend **1 Focus Point** to touch a creature and restore:

**Hit Points equal to 1 × Martial Arts die + Wisdom modifier**

---

### Flurry of Blows Synergy

When you use **Flurry of Blows**, you can **replace one of the Unarmed Strikes** with **Hand of Healing**, without expending a Focus Point for the healing.

---

## 3rd Level – Implements of Mercy

You gain proficiency in:

- **Insight**
- **Medicine**
- **Herbalism Kit**

---

## 6th Level – Physician’s Touch

Your healing and harming abilities improve:

### Hand of Harm

When you use **Hand of Harm**, you may also impose the **Poisoned condition** on the target until the **end of your next turn**.

---

### Hand of Healing

When you use **Hand of Healing**, you may also end **one of the following conditions** on the target:

- **Blinded**
- **Deafened**
- **Paralyzed**
- **Poisoned**
- **Stunned**

---

## 11th Level – Flurry of Healing and Harm

When you use **Flurry of Blows**, you may:

- **Replace each Unarmed Strike with Hand of Healing** (no Focus Point cost for healing).
- When you deal damage with a Flurry of Blows **Unarmed Strike**, you may also use **Hand of Harm** with that strike **without expending a Focus Point**.

You may still use **Hand of Harm only once per turn**.

---

### Uses

Use this feature a number of times equal to your **Wisdom modifier** (minimum of once).  
Regain all uses after a **Long Rest**.

---

## 17th Level – Hand of Ultimate Mercy

As a **Magic Action**, touch the corpse of a creature that died **within the past 24 hours** and expend **5 Focus Points**.  
The creature returns to life with:

**4d10 + Wisdom modifier Hit Points**

---

### Conditions Removed

If the creature died while **Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, Poisoned, or Stunned**, those conditions are removed upon revival.

---

### Uses

Once you use this feature, you cannot do so again until you finish a **Long Rest**.
