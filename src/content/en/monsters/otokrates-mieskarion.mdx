### Traits

**Multiattack.**  
Otokrates makes two melee weapon attacks with his **Perpetual Flameblade**.

**Perpetual Flameblade.**  
_Melee Spell Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 10.5 (3d6) fire damage. This weapon is always active and is treated as magical.

**Bloodlash Rebuke (Reaction).**  
When Otokrates is hit by a melee weapon attack, he may cast **Bloodlash Rebuke** as a reaction.  
The attacker takes **2d10 fire damage** and must succeed on a **DC 16 Strength saving throw** or be **pushed 10 feet away**.

**Ant rider**

Otokrates rides a [giant fire ant called Wildfire](/en/library/monsters/giant-fire-ant) as a mount.
