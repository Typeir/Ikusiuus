### Traits

**Scorching Ray.**  
_Ranged Spell Attack:_ +10 to hit, range 120 ft., up to 3 targets.  
_Hit:_ 22 (4d10) fire damage per ray.  
Mutated variant: **damage is doubled** compared to base spell.

**Scorching Fangs.**  
_Melee Spell Attack:_ +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., three targets.  
_Hit:_ 22 (4d10) damage, half fire and half acid.  
Each creature hit must succeed on a **DC 16 Constitution saving throw** or be **Paralyzed until the end of their next turn**.

**Oviposition (Recharge 6).**  
If a creature is **paralyzed**, Wildfire may implant a **burning egg**.  
The creature gains the **Incubating Inferno** condition (see below).

**Fireball (1/day).**  
Wildfire casts Fireball.  
20 ft. radius sphere, DC 16 Dexterity saving throw.  
On failure: **28 (8d6)** fire damage. Half on success.

**Grappler.**  
Wildfire may attempt to **grapple** as a **bonus action**.  
Grappled creatures are also **restrained**.

---

## Condition: Incubating Inferno

A creature affected by _Incubating Inferno_ suffers the following:

- Cannot reduce levels of **exhaustion** by any means
- Gains **vulnerability to fire damage**
- Gains **resistance to cold damage**
- The condition does **not deal damage over time**
- At the end of the creature’s next **short rest**, the egg **hatches**:
  - The creature takes **22 (4d10) fire damage**
  - Then makes a **DC 16 Constitution saving throw**
    - On failure: the creature is **Stunned for 1 minute** (save at end of each turn to end)  
      Only **Greater Restoration**, **Heal**, or **Wish** can remove the condition before hatching.
