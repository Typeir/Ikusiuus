## Traits

**Horse Mode.**  
As a bonus action, the kelpybara may enter quadrupedal acceleration until the end of its next turn. While in Horse Mode, it gains the following:

- Its movement speed increases by 20 feet
- It gains advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks
- It does not provoke opportunity attacks from creatures it moves past
- It ignores difficult terrain during movement
- It must drop its hand held equipment

**Luminous Blast (1/Short Rest).**  
The kelpybara emits a controlled flash from its lateral bulbs.  
All creatures within 15 feet must make a **DC 13 Wisdom saving throw** or suffer **disadvantage on attack rolls and Perception checks** until the end of their next turn.

**Ear-Shutter Reflex.**  
The kelpybara may use a reaction to fold its ears over its eyes, gaining advantage on saving throws against effects that cause blindness or rely on vision.

**Innate Spellcasting (Optional).**  
Some kelpybaras are capable of manifesting hostile arcane effects without a discernible source. The kelpybara may innately cast the following spells:

- [_Eldritch Blast_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:eldritch-blast) (Charisma +3 to hit)
- [_Arms of Hadar_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:arms-of-hadar) 1/day (DC 13)

**Opossable Thumbs.**  
The kelpybara has the ability to use its forelimbs as hands, allowing it to manipulate objects and use tools.

## Actions

**Gnash & Cling.**  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target  
_Hit:_ 12 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage  
If the target is Medium or smaller, it is grappled (escape DC 14).  
While grappling a creature:

- The kelpybara occupies the same space as the target
- The kelpybara has half cover against attacks from creatures other than the target
- The kelpybara may move the grappled creature with it at full speed

**Body Slam.**  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target  
_Hit:_ 16 (3d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage  
If the target is prone or grappled, it must succeed on a **DC 14 Strength saving throw** or be pushed 10 feet and knocked prone.

---

## Bonus Actions

**Fast Shove.**  
If the kelpybara hits a creature that is grappled or prone, it may immediately shove that creature 10 feet in any direction of it's choosing as a bonus action.
