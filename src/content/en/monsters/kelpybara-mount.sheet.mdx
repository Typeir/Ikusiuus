- **Senses**: Passive Perception 14, darkvision 60ft
- **Languages**: --
- **Challenge**: 2 (420 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +3

---

## Traits

- **Trampling Charge**
  - If the **Kelpybara** moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a **Jaws** attack, the creature becomes grappled unless it succeeds on a DC 15 Strength saving throw. Additionally, as a bonus action, the **Kelpybara** can attempt to restrain the creature and itself with a grapple (contested check).

---

## Actions

- **Jaws**: Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage.

---

## Spellcasting

The **Kelpybara** is a 4th-level warlock spellcaster. It has **two 2nd-level spell slots** and can cast the following:

- [_Darkness_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:darkness)
- [_Misty Step_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:misty-step)

It also knows the following **cantrips**:

- [_Eldritch Blast_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:eldritch-blast) (+3 to hit)

---

## Umbral Spellcasting

While obscured or under the night sky, the **Kelpybara** may ritually cast one of the following spells. Only one effect may be active at a time, and it may target only itself:

- [_Jump_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:jump)
- [_Water Walk_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:water-walk)
- [_Expeditious Retreat_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:expeditious-retreat)
