# Spear of Päimär (Forgery)

_Uncommon (requires attunement)_  
_Spear (Melee, Thrown, Versatile, Mastery: Flex)_  
_Can be used as a spellcasting focus when attuned_

A spear as weightless as light itself, crafted in **imitation** of the legendary Spear of Päimär. Though merely a forgery, it still carries a fraction of its intended power, transforming into a bolt of sunlight when hurled.

> “Even the lie of the sun can still burn.”

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type:** Spear (Melee, Simple, Thrown, Versatile, Special, Magical)
- **Damage:** 1d6 piercing (1d8)
- **Weight:** 5 lbs
- **Range:** 30/120 ft.

## Special Features

- **Sunborn Javelin** _(1/day, recharges at sunrise)_:  
  When thrown, the spear becomes a **bolt of sunlight**, forming a **5-foot-wide line** from you to a creature within 120 feet.
  - Each creature in that line (excluding you and the final target) must make a **DC 16 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **4d6 radiant damage** on a failure or half as much on success.
  - The spear reforms at the target's location.

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Flex** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Spears)_:  
  When using both hands to attack, gain **+1 bonus to damage rolls**.

# Spear of Päimär

_Rare (requires attunement)_  
_Spear +1 (Melee, Thrown, Versatile, Mastery: Flex)_  
_Can be used as a spellcasting focus when attuned_

A spear as weightless as light itself. Though only a fragment of the sun’s wrath remains, it transforms into a bolt of radiant fire when hurled with righteous intent.

> “We remember the sun best when it leaves a scar.”

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type:** Spear +1 (Melee, Simple, Thrown, Versatile, Special, Magical)
- **Damage:** 1d6 piercing (1d8)
- **Weight:** 5 lbs
- **Range:** 30/120 ft.

## Special Features

- **Lightspeed Hurl** _(1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rest if wielding with mastery)_:  
  When thrown as part of the **Attack action**, the spear ignites with solar speed:
  - On hit, it deals **+3d6 radiant damage**
  - After the attack (hit or miss), you may **teleport to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the spear**

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon**: This weapon has a **+1 bonus to attack and damage rolls** and is considered **magical for overcoming resistances and immunities**.

- **Spellcasting Focus**  
  May be used as a focus for casting spells and cantrips.

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Flex** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Spears)_:  
   When you make an attack with this weapon using both hands, you gain a **+1 bonus to damage rolls**.

- **Lightspeed Hurl** _(Mastery Skill — 1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rest if wielding with mastery)_:  
  When you throw this weapon as part of the **Attack action**, it blazes with solar velocity.
  - The attack deals an **additional 3d6 radiant damage** on hit.
  - Immediately after the attack lands (or misses), you may **teleport to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the spear**.

---

## Solar Cascade _(1/day, recharges at sunrise)_

Once per day, you may unleash the **Solar Cascade**:

- When hurled, the **Spear of Päimär** becomes a **beam of searing sunlight**, forming a **5 ft.-wide, 120 ft.-long line** from you to the target.
- Every creature in the line (excluding you and the final target) must succeed on a **DC 16 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **5d6 radiant damage** on a failed save, or **half as much** on a success.
- The spear **reforms at the target’s location**.

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Spear**: 1d6 piercing (1d8 versatile), 5 lb., **Thrown (30/120), Simple, Versatile**

# Spear of Päimär

_Very Rare (requires attunement)_  
_Spear +2 (Melee, Thrown, Versatile, Mastery: Flex)_  
_Can be used as a spellcasting focus when attuned_

The Spear of Päimär is a weapon of celestial justice. Once wielded by the champions who **slaughtered the divine**, its presence is both a blessing and a grim reminder. It gleams like a shard of the sun and whistles with holy velocity when thrown.

> “It is not the sun’s warmth we remember—but its spear.”

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type:** Spear +2 (Melee, Martial, Thrown, Versatile, Special, Magical)
- **Damage:** 1d6 piercing (1d8)
- **Weight:** 5 lbs
- **Range:** 60/240 ft.

## Special Features

- **Beacon of the Dawn**:  
  While attuned to this weapon, you emit **bright light in a 20-foot radius** and **dim light for 20 feet beyond that**.  
  You can suppress or resume this light as a **bonus action**.

- **Solar Cascade** _(1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rest with mastery)_:  
  When hurled, the spear becomes a **beam of divine sunlight**, forming a **5 ft.-wide line** up to **120 feet**:
  - Each creature in the line (excluding you and the final target) must make a **DC 17 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **6d8 radiant damage** on failure, or half on success.
  - On a hit, the final target takes an additional **3d8 radiant damage**.
  - The spear reforms in your hand at the start of your next turn.

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon**: This weapon has a **+2 bonus to attack and damage rolls** and is considered **magical for overcoming resistances and immunities**.

- **Spellcasting Focus**  
  May be used as a focus for casting spells and cantrips.

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Flex** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Spears)_:  
  When you make an attack with this weapon using both hands, you gain a **+1 bonus to damage rolls**.

- **Lightspeed Hurl** _(Mastery Skill — 1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rest if wielding with mastery)_:  
  When you throw this weapon as part of the **Attack action**, it blazes with solar velocity.
  - The attack deals an **additional 3d6 radiant damage** on hit.
  - Immediately after the attack lands (or misses), you may **teleport to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the spear**.
