# Warrior of the Elements

_Wield Strikes and Bursts of Elemental Power._

Warriors of the Elements tap into the power of the Elemental Planes. Harnessing their supernatural focus, these Monks momentarily tame the energy of the Elemental Chaos to empower themselves in and out of battle.

---

## 3rd Level – Elemental Attunement

At the **start of your turn**, you may expend **1 Focus Point** to imbue yourself with elemental energy.  
This energy lasts for **10 minutes**, or until you are **Incapacitated**.

---

### While Elemental Attunement is active:

#### Reach

Your **Unarmed Strike reach increases by 10 feet**.

---

#### Elemental Strikes

When you hit with an **Unarmed Strike**, you may choose to:

- Deal **Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder damage** instead of normal damage.

Additionally, when you deal one of these types, you may force the target to make a **Strength saving throw**:

- **Fail:** You may **move the target up to 10 feet** toward or away from you.

---

## 3rd Level – Manipulate Elements

You know the **[Elementalism](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:elementalism)** spell.

- **Wisdom** is your spellcasting ability for it.

---

## 6th Level – Elemental Burst

As a **Magic Action**, you may expend **2 Focus Points** to unleash a burst of elemental energy.

- Choose **Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder** damage.
- Create a **20-foot-radius Sphere** within **120 feet** of you.
- Each creature in the Sphere must make a **Dexterity saving throw**.

---

### Damage

- **Fail:** Take **3 × Martial Arts die** of the chosen damage type.
- **Success:** Take **half damage**.

---

## 11th Level – Stride of the Elements

While **Elemental Attunement** is active:

- You gain a **Fly Speed equal to your Speed**.
- You gain a **Swim Speed equal to your Speed**.

---

## 17th Level – Elemental Epitome

While **Elemental Attunement** is active, you gain the following benefits:

---

### Damage Resistance

You have **Resistance** to one of the following types of your choice:

- **Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder**

At the **start of each of your turns**, you may change this choice.

---

### Destructive Stride

When you use **Step of the Wind**, your **Speed increases by 20 feet** until the end of the turn.

For the duration, whenever you **enter a space within 5 feet of a creature of your choice**, that creature takes:

- **1 × Martial Arts die** of damage (Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder—your choice).

A creature can take this damage **only once per turn**.

---

### Empowered Strikes

Once per turn when you hit with an **Unarmed Strike**, you may deal additional damage equal to:

- **1 × Martial Arts die**

This extra damage is the **same type as the strike’s damage**.
