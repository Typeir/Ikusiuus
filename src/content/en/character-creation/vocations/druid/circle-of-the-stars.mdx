# Circle of the Stars

_Harness Secrets Hidden in Constellations._

The Circle of the Stars has tracked heavenly patterns since time immemorial, discovering secrets hidden amid the constellations. By understanding these secrets, Druids of this circle seek to harness the powers of the cosmos.

---

## 3rd Level – Star Map

You have created a **Star Map**, a Tiny object that serves as your **Spellcasting Focus** for Druid spells.  
Roll or choose its form from the table below:

| 1d6 | Map Form                                          |
| --- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | A scroll bearing depictions of constellations     |
| 2   | A stone tablet with fine holes drilled through it |
| 3   | An owlbear hide tooled with stellar symbols       |
| 4   | A collection of maps bound in an ebony cover      |
| 5   | A crystal engraved with starry patterns           |
| 6   | A glass disk etched with constellations           |

---

### Map Benefits

While holding the map:

- You always have **[Guidance](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:guidance)** and **[Guiding Bolt](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:guiding-bolt)** prepared.
- You can cast **Guiding Bolt** **without expending a spell slot** a number of times equal to your **Wisdom modifier** (minimum of once).
- You regain all uses when you finish a **Long Rest**.

---

### Replacement

If you lose the map, you can create a new one by performing a **1-hour ceremony** during a **Short or Long Rest**.  
This destroys the previous map.

---

## 3rd Level – Starry Form

As a **Bonus Action**, expend a use of **Wild Shape** to enter a **Starry Form** instead of transforming into a Beast.

- You retain your game statistics.
- Your body glimmers with constellations.
- You shed **Bright Light in a 10-foot radius** and **Dim Light for an additional 10 feet**.
- The form lasts for **10 minutes**, or until you **dismiss it (no action required)**, become **Incapacitated**, or use this feature again.

---

### Choose a Constellation

Each time you activate **Starry Form**, choose one of the following constellations:

---

#### Archer

- As part of the activation, and as a **Bonus Action** on subsequent turns, you can make a **ranged spell attack** against a target within **60 feet**.
- On a hit, deal **Radiant damage equal to 1d8 + your Wisdom modifier**.

---

#### Chalice

- When you cast a **healing spell** using a spell slot, you or another creature within **30 feet** regain additional **Hit Points equal to 1d8 + your Wisdom modifier**.

---

#### Dragon

- When you make an **Intelligence or Wisdom check** or a **Constitution saving throw to maintain Concentration**, treat a roll of **9 or lower as a 10**.

---

## 6th Level – Cosmic Omen

When you finish a **Long Rest**, consult your **Star Map** and roll a die.  
Until your next Long Rest, you gain one of the following **Reactions**, depending on the roll:

---

### Weal (Even Roll)

When a creature within **30 feet** makes a **d20 Test**, you may use your **Reaction** to roll **1d6** and **add the result** to the roll.

---

### Woe (Odd Roll)

When a creature within **30 feet** makes a **d20 Test**, you may use your **Reaction** to roll **1d6** and **subtract the result** from the roll.

---

### Uses

You can use this **Reaction** a number of times equal to your **Wisdom modifier** (minimum of once).  
Regain all uses after a **Long Rest**.

---

## 10th Level – Twinkling Constellations

Your **Starry Form** improves:

- The **Archer** and **Chalice** dice become **2d8**.
- While in the **Dragon** form, you gain a **Fly Speed of 20 feet** and can **hover**.
- At the **start of each of your turns**, you may change which constellation is active.

---

## 14th Level – Full of Stars

While in **Starry Form**, you become **partially incorporeal**.

- Gain **Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage**.
